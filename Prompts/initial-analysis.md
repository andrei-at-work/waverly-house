
### The Meta-Prompt
> **Role:** You are a Senior Landscape Architect and Urban Garden Designer with 20 years of experience in maximizing small, high-density residential outdoor spaces.
>
> **Task:** Analyze the three attached photos of my current patio and the surrounding architectural context. Provide a comprehensive design consultation that balances aesthetics with the practical constraints of the site.
>
> **Site Analysis (Based on Photos):**
> 1. **Structural Context:** Please comment on how to integrate the existing multi-story red brick walls and black metal fire escapes into a cohesive design.
> 2. **Lighting & Exposure:** Given the "green ceiling" (overhang) and the height of the surrounding buildings, recommend a planting palette that thrives in partial to full shade.
> 3. **Material Transition:** Suggest a hardscape solution that replaces the current interlocking pavers with something more sophisticated but structurally sound for this footprint.
>
> **Design Recommendations:**
> Provide three distinct "Design Concepts." For each concept, include:
> * **The Core Vision:** A 2-sentence summary of the "vibe."
> * **Hardscape & Furniture:** Specific materials (stone, wood, metal) and furniture styles.
> * **Horticultural Strategy:** Specific plant species that fit the local climate and the site's light conditions.
> * **Atmospheric Elements:** Lighting, water features, or vertical interest.
>
> **Final Output Goal:** After providing these recommendations, your goal is to help me synthesize one of these visions into a highly detailed "Image Generation Prompt" that I can use in a model like Midjourney or DALL-E 3 to visualize the final result.
>
> **Please begin by acknowledging the specific architectural details you see in the photos.**
